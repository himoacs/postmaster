The Story

Why PostMaster Exists

Built by a writer frustrated with generic AI content.

The Problem with AI Writing

When I started using AI to help with writing, I kept running into the same frustrating problem that the content sounded generic. It had that unmistakable AI quality that people call "AI slop." The tone was off, the phrasing felt cookie-cutter, and most importantly, it didn't sound like me. I mean how many posts have you seen that mention "The Key Takeaway?" ?

I realized I wasn't alone. Many writers want to leverage AI's capabilities such as its speed, its ability to handle research, its knack for generating variations but they don't want to sacrifice their voice or settle for mediocre output.

That's why I built PostMaster. I wanted a tool that would let me tap into multiple AI models, allow me to iterate with AI, avoid using common AI pharases, and leverage my personal writing style. Your unique voice is a competitive advantage, don't lose it to AI.
