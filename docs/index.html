Why PostMaster?

Multi-Model Generation

Send your prompt to multiple AI models simultaneously and compare their outputs side by side.

Smart Synthesis

Combine the best parts of each output into a single polished piece with AI-powered synthesis.

Style Learning

Analyze your existing writing samples and train the AI to match your unique voice and tone.

Privacy First

Everything runs locally. Your API keys and content never leave your machine.

Knowledge Base

Import URLs, documents, and notes. Reference them to generate contextually accurate content.

Fact Checking

Verify claims in your content against your knowledge base with built-in fact checking.

How It Works

1

Add Your API Keys

Connect OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, or any provider. Your keys are encrypted locally.

2

Write Your Prompt

Describe what you want to write. Add references from your knowledge base if needed.

3

Select Models

Choose 2-5 models to generate content, or use YOLO mode for automatic selection.

4

Compare & Synthesize

Review outputs side by side, then synthesize the best parts into your final piece.
