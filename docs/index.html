The workflow

How PostMaster Works

A deliberate approach to AI-assisted writing that keeps you in control.

01

Compare Models

Submit your prompt once, receive outputs from multiple AI models. See how each interprets your request.

02

Synthesize Results

Pick the strongest elements from each output. Combine them into a cohesive draft that captures your intent.

03

Refine & Iterate

Provide feedback, adjust tone, tighten phrasing. Iterate until every sentence feels intentional.

04

Learn Your Style

Share examples of your writing. PostMaster analyzes your voice to generate content that sounds like you.

05

Generate Images

Create matching visuals with DALL-E or Stable Diffusion, integrated directly into your workflow.

06

Use Your Own Keys

Bring your API keys for complete control. No subscriptions, just pay for what you use.
